Portfolio Accounting is now the highest MVP priority.

Do NOT build any new features.

Do NOT change UI.

Do NOT add analytics, charts, watchlists, market intelligence, reports, or cosmetic enhancements.

Task: Audit and strengthen the Test Runner coverage for portfolio accounting and valuation integrity.

Add automated tests for the following critical scenarios:

A1 – Buy Transaction

* Buy new position.
* Verify quantity, average cost, market value, unrealized P&L.

A2 – Multiple Buys

* Buy same asset multiple times at different prices.
* Verify weighted average cost is correct.

A3 – Partial Sell

* Sell part of a position.
* Verify:
    * Remaining quantity
    * Remaining cost basis
    * Realized gain/loss
    * Unrealized gain/loss

A4 – Full Close

* Sell entire position.
* Verify:
    * Position removed from active holdings
    * Moved to Closed Holdings
    * Realized gain/loss preserved

A5 – Multi-Currency Valuation

* Holdings in SAR and USD.
* Verify portfolio valuation changes correctly when base currency changes.

A6 – Valuation Consistency
Verify Holdings, Accounts, Allocation, Risk, and Command Center all use the same valuation engine and produce identical portfolio totals.

A7 – Cash Integrity

* Buy decreases cash.
* Sell increases cash.
* Portfolio value reconciliation remains correct.

A8 – Closed Holdings Integrity

* Historical realized P&L remains unchanged after close.
* Closed positions do not affect active holdings calculations.

A9 – Data Persistence

* Save data.
* Restart app.
* Verify holdings, accounts, transactions, and realized gains remain unchanged.

A10 – Regression Protection
Create blocker-level tests that fail the suite if:

* Portfolio totals differ across modules.
* Quantity becomes negative.
* Cost basis becomes invalid.
* Realized and unrealized gains become inconsistent.
* Closed holdings reappear in active holdings.

Deliver:

1. Full list of new tests added.
2. PASS/FAIL status for each test.
3. Any accounting defects discovered.
4. Recommended fixes before further feature development.

Do not implement new functionality unless a test exposes a defect.
Focus on verification and data integrity only.
